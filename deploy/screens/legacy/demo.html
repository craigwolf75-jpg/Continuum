The problem
The visit ends. The recovery doesn't.
Care gets the worker to a doctor quickly. That part works. Then they go home, and for weeks no one can see how the recovery is actually going. Restrictions get forgotten. Light duty never gets arranged. A claim quietly drifts.
63 days
