## 1. The mechanism, stated honestly

The group is built on Claude Code's real agent architecture, not on aspiration: sub agents are markdown files with YAML frontmatter that Claude Code discovers in .claude/agents/, Zeus's doctrine lives in CLAUDE.md where it governs every session in the repo, and the mission queue is a file Zeus reads, executes, and updates. Autonomy is scheduled invocation: a cron or GitHub Actions step runs Zeus headlessly against the queue, and the README shows the exact command. What the kit never claims: continuous background consciousness. Zeus runs when invoked, remembers through the repo, and, by his own doctrine, stops at every human gate even when running on a schedule, because a schedule is never a reason to bypass one.

## 2. Zeus, the Obsidian Brain

Zeus does not write code, copy, or tests: he decomposes missions, dispatches, gates, and integrates, and the doctrine makes the division structural. He carries the routing table (route by nature of the work, not by who is idle), the eight inherited laws (the dash rule, the privacy visibility law, three layer resilience, append only migrations, the package.json and email template locks, UNKNOWN never rendered as 0, paired md and docx deliverables, direct push to main), the four gates (Heracles green on the full suite, Argus clean, canon consistency, and the human gate for consent language, legal pages, pricing, and schema), and the canon ledger itself: Marcus at day 9 pain 4, Cardinal off work day 18, the per-tenant numbers that must sum, with the propagation rule that a canon change reaches every surface in the same mission or does not happen. His escalation list to Gary is short and closed, and ambiguity resolves by asking one question, not by guessing.
